About Us

CakeDrop is a small handmade bakery that specializes in making delicious cakes for every occasion. Located in the heart of the community of San Joaquin, our bakery is a place where moments are celebrated and memories are made. Each cake is handcrafted with attention to detail, ensuring a truly memorable experience for our customers. From birthdays and weddings to anniversaries and special events, we ensure that every cake reflects the style of our customers.
